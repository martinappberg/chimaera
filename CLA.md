## How to sign

The signing is handled automatically by a bot on your pull request. When you
open your first PR, the CLA bot will comment with a link to this document and
ask you to sign by posting a one-line comment on the PR:

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

Your GitHub username and the pull request are recorded as your signature in
`signatures/version1/cla.json` on the `cla-signatures` branch. You sign once;
the bot remembers you for every future contribution. If the CLA text changes, a
new signature version is created and you will be asked to sign again.
